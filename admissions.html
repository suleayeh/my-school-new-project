Admission Requirements

Submit the following documents and information for a successful application.

Completed Application

Fill the online admission form and attach the required passport photograph.

Academic Records

Provide previous school report cards and any national examination scores if available.

Guardian Details

Include parent or guardian contact information and proof of residency in Kano State.

Admission Process

Our simple process helps families apply quickly and track application progress.

1. Apply Online

Complete the online application form with student and guardian details.

2. Submit Documents

Upload a passport photograph, previous school record, and any supporting documents.

3. Interview

Attend a brief screening interview with our admissions officer.
